**Complete Install Workflow & Initial Assessment Report**

## Part 2: Complete the Install Workflow

### Step 4: Select a Job
- From the Tech Dashboard, click on any job card to start the Install workflow
- The workflow will open with **14 sequential steps**

---

### Install Workflow Steps (Complete Each in Order)

#### **Step 1: Office Preparation** (Optional)
- Review customer profile and insurance info
- Note any special instructions
- Click **"Next"** to continue

#### **Step 2: Property Arrival**
- Clock in with arrival time
- Select arrival window (9-1, 12-4, or custom)
- Capture exterior property photo
- Click **"Next"**

#### **Step 3: Customer Introduction**
- Document customer walkthrough
- Review ground rules checklist:
  - Access to water/restroom
  - Pet/security information
  - Parking location
  - Contents/valuables discussion
- Click **"Next"**

#### **Step 4: Cause of Loss**
- Document the water damage source:
  - Select cause type (e.g., "Supply Line Leak")
  - Enter description and location
  - Set event date and discovery date
- Document water category (Cat 1, 2, or 3) and water class (1-4)
- Capture cause of loss photo
- Click **"Next"**

#### **Step 5: Room Assessment**
- **Add each affected room:**
  - Enter room name (e.g., "Master Bathroom")
  - Measure dimensions (length × width × height)
  - Take moisture readings and document wet materials
  - Photograph pre-existing damage if present
  - Capture assessment photos
- **Repeat for all affected rooms**
- Click **"Next"**

#### **Step 6: Define Drying Chambers**
- Group rooms into drying zones
- Each chamber gets its own dehumidifier
- Example: Group "Master Bath + Master Bedroom" as Chamber 1
- Click **"Next"**

#### **Step 7: Equipment Calculation**
- System automatically calculates IICRC-compliant equipment needs:
  - Air movers per room (based on cubic footage)
  - Dehumidifiers per chamber (based on total water class)
- Review calculations
- Click **"Next"**

#### **Step 8: Partial Demo** (Optional)
- Document any demo work performed during install:
  - Material type removed (drywall, baseboard, etc.)
  - Quantity removed
  - Room location
  - Capture demo photos
- Skip if no demo performed
- Click **"Next"**

#### **Step 9: Equipment Placement**
- **For each room:**
  - Scan equipment QR codes or enter serial numbers
  - Assign air movers to rooms
  - Assign dehumidifiers to chambers
  - Document placement with photos
- Click **"Next"**

#### **Step 10: Additional Billable Work**
- Log any additional services:
  - Emergency water extraction
  - Content manipulation
  - Antimicrobial application
  - Specialty drying (hardwood, etc.)
- Enter quantities and notes
- Click **"Next"**

#### **Step 11: Final Photos**
- Capture equipment setup photos:
  - Overall room views showing equipment placement
  - Dehumidifier placement in each chamber
  - Any special equipment configurations
- Click **"Next"**

#### **Step 12: Schedule Work**
- Plan future visits:
  - **Day 2:** Check Service (moisture readings)
  - **Day 3+:** Additional check services
  - **Final Day:** Equipment Pull
- Enter estimated drying days (typically 3-5 days)
- Click **"Next"**

#### **Step 13: Communicate Plan**
- Review complete mitigation plan with tech
- Confirm next visit date/time
- **Note:** This is internal communication, not sent to customer
- Click **"Next"**

#### **Step 14: Complete**
- Review workflow summary
- Clock out with departure time
- Click **"Complete Install"**
- You'll be returned to the Tech Dashboard

## Part 3: View Initial Assessment Report in PSM Dashboard

### Step 15: Logout from Tech Account
- Click the logout button in the Tech Dashboard
- You'll be returned to the login screen

### Step 16: Login as PSM
Use the PSM demo credentials:

- **Email:** `psm@demo.com`
- **Password:** `password123`

### Step 17: Access PSM Dashboard
- After login, you'll see the **PSM Dashboard**
- This shows all jobs across all phases
- You'll see job cards with status indicators

### Step 18: Locate Your Completed Job
- Find the job you just completed in the Install workflow
- Look for jobs with status **"Field Complete - Reviewing"**
- Jobs with red/orange left borders indicate critical/high priority flags

### Step 19: Open Job Detail View
- Click on the job card to open the full detail view
- You'll see multiple panels:
  - **Red Flag Dashboard** (left panel)
  - **Presentation Panel** (center - this is your report)
  - **PSM Actions Panel** (right panel)

### Step 20: View Initial Assessment Report
The **Presentation Panel** contains 8 tabs with comprehensive documentation:

#### **Tab 1: Overview**
- Customer information and property details
- Cause of loss summary
- Water category and class
- Total affected square footage
- Key dates (loss date, discovery date, install date)

#### **Tab 2: Before/After**
- Exterior property photo
- Cause of loss photo
- Room-by-room before photos
- Photo gallery with filtering

#### **Tab 3: Rooms**
- Detailed breakdown of each affected room:
  - Dimensions and square footage
  - Moisture readings with dry standards
  - Affected materials (wet vs. removed)
  - Pre-existing damage documentation

#### **Tab 4: Equipment**
- Complete equipment inventory:
  - Air movers by room (serial numbers)
  - Dehumidifiers by chamber (serial numbers)
  - Equipment placement photos
- IICRC calculation justification

#### **Tab 5: Demo**
- Materials removed during install
- Quantities and locations
- Before/after demo photos

#### **Tab 6: Timeline**
- Install visit details (arrival/departure times)
- Scheduled future visits (check services, pull)
- Estimated project completion date

#### **Tab 7: Financials**
- Billable items summary:
  - Equipment rental days
  - Labor hours
  - Additional services
- Cost projections

#### **Tab 8: Documents**
- Downloadable reports
- Customer signatures
- Insurance documentation

### Step 21: Generate Printable Report
- Scroll to the bottom of the Presentation Panel
- Click **"Generate Initial Inspection Report"** button
- A new window opens with a formatted, printable report
- Click the **Print** button to save as PDF or print

The report includes:
- Complete property and loss information
- Room-by-room moisture readings
- Equipment calculations and placement
- IICRC S500 compliance documentation
- Photos and documentation
- Professional formatting ready for insurance submission
