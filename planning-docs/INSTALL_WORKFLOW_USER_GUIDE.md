# Drylogs Install Workflow - User Guide

**Quick Reference Guide for Field Technicians**

---

## Getting Started

### Login

1. Open the Drylogs app in your browser
2. Enter your **email** and **password**
3. Tap **Sign In**
4. You'll see the **Tech Dashboard** with your assigned jobs

---

## Starting an Install Workflow

### From Dashboard

1. Find your assigned job in the job list
2. Tap **Start Install** on the job card
3. The install workflow begins at Step 1

---

## Install Workflow Steps (14 Steps)

### Step 1: Office Preparation (Optional)

- Review job details before departing
- Check customer name, address, and cause of loss
- Tap **Continue** when ready

---

### Step 2: Property Arrival

**What to do:**
1. Enter **arrival time** (auto-fills with current time)
2. Tap **ðŸ“· Take Photo** to capture front door/exterior
3. Confirm arrival location

**Tip:** This documents when you arrived on site.

---

### Step 3: Customer Introduction

**What to do:**
1. Complete the introduction checklist:
   - Introduced yourself
   - Explained ground rules
   - Completed walkthrough
   - Answered questions
   - Checked utilities
2. Mark any concerns:
   - Vulnerable occupants
   - Building age (pre-1980)
   - Other safety notes
3. Add notes if needed
4. Tap **Continue**

**Tip:** This ensures proper customer communication.

---

### Step 4: Cause of Loss

**What to do:**
1. Document the water source:
   - Select **type** (pipe burst, roof leak, etc.)
   - Choose **water category** (Cat 1, 2, or 3)
2. Tap **ðŸ“· Take Photos** of the source/damage
3. Add notes about the incident
4. Tap **Continue**

**Tip:** Accurate cause documentation is critical for claims.

---

### Step 5: Room Assessment (Most Important Step)

**For each affected room:**

#### Basic Info Tab:
1. Enter **room name** (e.g., "Master Bedroom")
2. Enter **dimensions** (length Ã— width Ã— height)
3. Select **floor level** and **room type**
4. Enter **affected square footage** for floor/walls/ceiling

#### Moisture Tab:
1. Tap **+ Add Material Reading**
2. Select material and enter moisture %
3. Take moisture reading photos
4. Repeat for all affected materials

#### Materials Tab:
1. **Check materials present in the room**
2. **For materials to remove:** Check the "Remove" box
3. Enter square footage for materials being removed
4. Select removal reason (water damage, mold, etc.)
5. Add notes if needed

**CRITICAL:** Material selections save automatically but verify they're checked before moving to next room.

#### Pre-existing Damage Tab:
1. Document any pre-existing issues
2. Take photos of pre-existing damage
3. Add description

#### Photos Tab:
1. Tap **ðŸ“· Capture Photos**
2. Take minimum **4 overall room photos**
3. Photos save automatically

**Tip:** Complete ALL tabs before saving the room. Take your time - this data feeds the entire report.

---

### Step 6: Define Drying Chambers

**What to do:**
1. Tap **+ Create Chamber**
2. Enter chamber name (e.g., "Chamber 1 - Upstairs")
3. Select rooms that belong to this chamber
4. Chambers group rooms that will dry together
5. Tap **Save Chamber**
6. Repeat for additional chambers

**Tip:** Chambers help calculate equipment needs.

---

### Step 7: Partial Demo (Optional)

**If performing demo during install:**
1. Select the room
2. Tap **+ Add Material Removed**
3. Enter material type and quantity
4. Select removal reason
5. Tap **ðŸ“· Take Photos** of demo work
6. Add notes about demo work

**Tip:** Skip this if no demo performed during install.

---

### Step 8: Equipment Calculation & Placement

**Equipment Calculation:**
1. The app automatically calculates equipment based on:
   - Room cubic footage
   - Damage class
   - Chambers defined
2. Review the equipment requirements

**Equipment Placement:**
1. Tap **Scan & Place Equipment**
2. Select the chamber
3. Scan equipment barcode or enter serial number
4. Select room where equipment is placed
5. Repeat for all equipment

**Tip:** The app tells you what you need - just scan and place.

---

### Step 9: Additional Billable Work (Optional)

**If you performed extra services:**
1. Tap **+ Add Billable Work**
2. Select service type (e.g., "Containment", "Pack-out")
3. Enter quantity and rate
4. Add description
5. Tap **Save**

**Tip:** Document everything billable for accurate invoicing.

---

### Step 10: Final Photos

**What to do:**
1. Tap **ðŸ“· Capture Photos**
2. Take photos showing:
   - Equipment setup in each room
   - Overall job site
   - Key areas of concern
3. Minimum **4 photos** required
4. Tap **Continue** when done

**Tip:** Show the complete equipment setup clearly.

---

### Step 11: Schedule Work

**What to do:**
1. Tap **+ Add Visit** to schedule Day 2 and beyond
2. For each visit:
   - Select date and time
   - Choose visit type (monitoring, daily check, final removal)
   - Add notes about what will be done
3. Tap **Save Schedule**

**Tip:** Plan the full drying schedule now.

---

### Step 12: Communicate Plan

**Review with customer:**
- **Rooms Assessed:** Shows all rooms documented
- **Equipment Placed:** Lists all equipment by chamber/room
- **Demo Work:** Shows materials marked for removal
- **Schedule:** Displays planned visits
- **Contact Info:** Your contact information

**What to do:**
1. Review the plan on-screen with customer
2. Explain the drying process and timeline
3. Answer customer questions
4. Tap **Continue** when discussion complete

**Tip:** This is your chance to set expectations clearly.

---

### Step 13: Complete Install

**Final step:**
1. Enter **departure time** (auto-fills)
2. Add any **tech notes** (optional)
3. Tap **Complete Install Workflow**

**What happens:**
- All data saves to Firebase
- Job phase updates to "Install Complete"
- You return to the Tech Dashboard
- Initial Inspection Report becomes available to office staff

## Viewing Initial Inspection Report (PSM Dashboard)

**For Office Staff Only:**

### Accessing PSM Dashboard

1. Log in with PSM credentials
2. Click **PSM Dashboard** in navigation
3. Find the completed job in job list

### Generate Initial Inspection Report

1. Look for the job card
2. You'll see **"Generate Initial Inspection Report"** button
   - Only appears after install is completed
3. Tap the button
4. Report opens in new window with:
   - Entrusted Restoration logo
   - Customer and job details
   - Complete room assessments
   - Moisture readings and photos
   - Materials marked for removal
   - Equipment placed
   - Drying schedule

## Tips for Success

### Room Assessment
- **Take your time** - this is the most important step
- Mark materials for removal carefully
- Take clear moisture reading photos
- Document pre-existing damage thoroughly

### Photos
- Take MORE than the minimum
- Ensure good lighting
- Photos save automatically in background
- Gallery shows all photos from entire workflow

### Equipment
- Scan all equipment placed
- Double-check room assignments
- Verify equipment totals match calculation

### Communication
- Review the plan step thoroughly with customer
- Set clear expectations about drying timeline
- Give customer your contact info

---

## Troubleshooting

### Materials not saving
- Materials now save immediately when checked
- If you mark materials and navigate away, they're saved
- Verify checkmarks are visible before leaving room

### Photos not showing in gallery
- Photos upload in background automatically
- Check notification area for upload status
- Photos appear in gallery once uploaded

### Can't complete workflow
- Review validation errors shown
- Tap workflow header to see all steps
- Navigate to incomplete steps
- Equipment placement is optional (warning only)
